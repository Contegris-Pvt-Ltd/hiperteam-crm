# Getting Started with IntelliSales CRM

Welcome to **IntelliSales CRM** — a complete Business & People Operating System designed to manage every stage of your customer lifecycle, from first contact to long-term success.

## What is IntelliSales CRM?

IntelliSales CRM is a multi-tenant SaaS platform that unifies your entire business workflow into a single, integrated system. Rather than juggling separate tools for sales, project management, invoicing, and customer support, IntelliSales CRM brings everything together under one roof.

The platform follows a natural business lifecycle:

**Lead → Opportunity → Deal → Project → Support → Customer Success**

Every module is interconnected, so data flows seamlessly from one stage to the next. When you convert a lead, it automatically creates a contact, account, and opportunity. When you close a deal, you can spin up a project with a single click.

## Key Modules Overview

| Module | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Real-time KPIs, charts, and widgets tailored to your role |
| **Contacts** | Manage individuals — your people directory |
| **Accounts** | Manage organizations (B2B and B2C) |
| **Leads** | Track and qualify inbound and outbound prospects |
| **Opportunities** | Manage qualified deals through your sales pipeline |
| **Products** | Maintain your product/service catalog and price books |
| **Invoices** | Create, send, and track invoices and payments |
| **Projects** | Plan and execute projects with phases, tasks, and Gantt charts |
| **Tasks** | Manage to-dos, calls, meetings, and follow-ups |
| **Email Inbox** | Integrated email with CRM entity linking |
| **Reports** | Build custom reports with charts, filters, and scheduling |
| **Workflows** | Automate repetitive actions with trigger-based rules |
| **Approvals** | Route records through approval chains |
| **Forms** | Build public-facing forms to capture leads and data |
| **Scheduling** | Publish booking pages for clients to schedule meetings |
| **Notifications** | Stay informed with in-app, email, SMS, and push alerts |

## System Requirements

IntelliSales CRM is a cloud-based application. You need:

- A **modern web browser** — Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari (latest two major versions)
- A **stable internet connection**
- A screen resolution of **1280x720 or higher** (1920x1080 recommended)

:::tip
For the best experience, use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge on a desktop or laptop computer. The interface is responsive and works on tablets, but a larger screen makes managing complex data much easier.
:::

## Quick Start Guide

Follow these steps to get oriented after your first login:

1. **Log in** to your tenant workspace using the credentials provided by your administrator. See [Logging In](./logging-in.md) for detailed instructions.

2. **Explore the Dashboard** — your landing page shows key metrics and activity summaries. See [Dashboard Overview](./dashboard-overview.md).

3. **Navigate the sidebar** — the left sidebar contains links to every module. Hover over icons to see labels, or expand the sidebar for full text. See [Navigating the Interface](./navigating-the-interface.md).

4. **Check your profile** — click your avatar in the top-right corner to review your profile, set your avatar, and configure notification preferences. See [Your Profile](./your-profile.md).

5. **Start working** — depending on your role, you might:
   - **Sales reps:** Start with [Leads](./leads-overview.md) or [Opportunities](./opportunities-overview.md)
   - **Managers:** Explore [Reports](./reports-overview.md) and [Dashboard Widgets](./dashboard-widgets.md)
   - **Support staff:** Check [Tasks](./tasks-overview.md) and [Notifications](./notifications-overview.md)

:::info
Your administrator controls which modules you can access and what actions you can perform. If you cannot see a module or button mentioned in this guide, contact your admin to check your role permissions.
:::

## Getting Help

- Use the **global search bar** at the top of the page to quickly find any record across modules. See [Global Search](./global-search.md).
- Check the **notification bell** for updates on records assigned to you.
- Reach out to your organization's IntelliSales CRM administrator for role or permission questions.
