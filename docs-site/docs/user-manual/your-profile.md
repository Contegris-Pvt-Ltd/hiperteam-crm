# Your Profile

Your profile contains your personal information, avatar, and preferences within HiperTeam CRM. Keeping it up to date helps your team identify you and ensures communications are routed correctly.

## Viewing Your Profile

To view your profile:

1. Click your **avatar** or initials in the top-right corner of the header bar.
2. Select **Profile** from the dropdown menu.

Your profile page displays your name, email, phone number, role, department, and team assignments.

![Screenshot: User profile page showing personal information and avatar](../../static/img/screenshots/profile/profile-page.png)

## Editing Profile Information

To update your profile details:

1. Navigate to your profile page.
2. Click the **Edit** button.
3. Update any of the following fields:
   - **First Name** and **Last Name**
   - **Email Address**
   - **Phone Number**
   - **Job Title**
4. Click **Save** to apply your changes.

:::note
Some fields may be locked by your administrator and cannot be edited from your profile. Contact your admin if you need to change a restricted field.
:::

## Uploading an Avatar

Your avatar appears throughout the platform — in the header, on records you own, in activity timelines, and in team views.

To upload or change your avatar:

1. On your profile page, click the **camera icon** overlaying your current avatar (or the placeholder circle).
2. Select an image file from your computer (JPEG, PNG, or GIF).
3. The image uploads automatically and replaces your previous avatar.

:::tip
Use a clear, professional headshot at least 200x200 pixels. The image will be cropped to a circle in most places it appears.
:::

![Screenshot: Avatar upload area with camera icon overlay](../../static/img/screenshots/profile/avatar-upload.png)

## Changing Your Password

To change your password:

1. Navigate to your profile page.
2. Click **Change Password** (or find the password section).
3. Enter your **current password**.
4. Enter your **new password**.
5. Confirm the new password.
6. Click **Update Password**.

:::warning
After changing your password, your current session remains active. However, any other devices or browsers where you were logged in may require you to sign in again with the new password.
:::

## Email Signature Setup

You can configure an email signature that is appended to emails sent from the integrated inbox:

1. Navigate to your profile or personal settings.
2. Find the **Email Signature** section.
3. Enter your signature text (your name, title, phone number, company details).
4. Click **Save**.

Your signature will automatically appear at the bottom of new emails and replies composed from the [Email Inbox](./email-inbox.md).

## Notification Preferences

You can control which notifications you receive and through which channels:

1. From your profile or the **Settings** menu, navigate to **Notification Preferences**.
2. For each notification type, toggle the channels you want:
   - **In-App** — notifications within the platform
   - **Email** — notifications sent to your email
   - **SMS** — text message notifications
   - **Browser Push** — desktop push notifications
3. Save your preferences.

For full details on how notifications work, see [Notifications](./notifications-overview.md).

![Screenshot: Notification preferences grid with channel toggles per event type](../../static/img/screenshots/profile/notification-preferences.png)

:::info
Administrators can set organization-wide notification defaults. Your personal preferences override the defaults where allowed.
:::
