# Contact Detail Page

The contact detail page gives you a comprehensive view of an individual contact and all their related information. It is the central hub for managing your relationship with that person.

## Contact Detail Layout

At the top of the page, you will see:

- **Avatar** — the contact's profile photo (or initials placeholder)
- **Full Name** — first and last name displayed prominently
- **Job Title and Company** — professional context
- **Status badge** — current contact status
- **Email and Phone** — quick-access contact information with click-to-copy
- **Action buttons** — Edit, Delete, and other actions

## Tabs

The contact detail page organizes related information into tabs:

### Activity
A chronological timeline of all interactions with this contact — calls, emails, meetings, stage changes, and system events. See [Activity Timeline](./activity-timeline.md) for details.

### Emails
All email threads involving this contact. You can view, reply to, and compose new emails directly from this tab. See [Email Inbox](./email-inbox.md).

### Notes
Free-text notes added by team members about this contact. Add notes to record conversation summaries, observations, or action items. See [Notes & Documents](./notes-documents.md).

### Accounts
Shows all **accounts (organizations)** linked to this contact. Each link includes a **role** indicating the contact's relationship to the account:

| Role | Description |
|---|---|
| **Employee** | Works at the organization |
| **Decision Maker** | Has authority to approve deals |
| **Influencer** | Influences buying decisions |
| **Executive Sponsor** | Senior leader backing the relationship |
| **Technical Contact** | Technical point of contact |
| **Billing Contact** | Handles invoicing and payments |
| **Custom** | Any other role defined by your org |

A contact can be linked to **multiple accounts** with different roles.

:::tip
Linking contacts to accounts with accurate roles helps your team understand organizational relationships at a glance. This is especially valuable for B2B sales where multiple stakeholders are involved in decisions.
:::

### Tasks
Tasks assigned to or related to this contact. You can create new tasks directly from this tab. See [Tasks Overview](./tasks-overview.md).

### Documents
Files uploaded and attached to this contact — contracts, proposals, identification documents, etc. See [Notes & Documents](./notes-documents.md).

### History
A detailed audit log showing every change made to the contact record — what changed, the old value, the new value, who made the change, and when. This provides full traceability.

## Uploading Avatar

To add or change the contact's avatar:

1. Click the **avatar circle** (or camera icon overlay) at the top of the detail page.
2. Select an image file from your computer.
3. The avatar updates immediately.

## Profile Completion Indicator

A **profile completion** indicator may display as a percentage or progress bar, showing how many fields have been filled in for this contact. A more complete profile helps your team make better decisions.

:::note
The fields that count toward completion are configured by your administrator and may include both standard and custom fields.
:::

## Custom Field Groups

Below the main detail section, you will see **collapsible field groups** containing custom fields configured by your administrator. Each group has a header you can click to expand or collapse.

Groups might include:
- Marketing Preferences
- Financial Information
- Internal Notes
- Custom Attributes

## Linking Contacts to Accounts

To link a contact to an account:

1. Go to the **Accounts** tab on the contact detail page.
2. Click **Link Account** (or the "+" button).
3. Search for and select the account.
4. Choose the contact's **role** at that account.
5. Optionally mark it as the **primary** account.
6. Click **Save**.

A contact can have one primary account and multiple secondary accounts.

## Admin-Controlled Layout

The layout of the contact detail page — which fields appear, their order, and which groups they belong to — is managed by your administrator through the **Layout Designer**. If you need fields rearranged or new sections added, contact your admin.
