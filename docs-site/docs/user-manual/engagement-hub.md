# Engagement Hub

The Engagement Hub is your unified platform for lead capture and meeting scheduling. Access it from the sidebar under **Engagement**.

![Screenshot: Engagement Hub main page](../../static/img/screenshots/user/engagement-hub.png)

The hub organizes two powerful tools under one roof:

## Forms

Create custom web forms to capture leads, collect feedback, or gather information. Forms can be:
- **Standard Forms** — Collect data and trigger CRM actions (create leads, contacts, accounts)
- **Meeting Booking Pages** — Let visitors book meetings directly on your calendar

Navigate to **Engagement → Forms** to manage all your forms.

:::tip
Forms support embedding on external websites via iframe, JavaScript snippet, or popup modal. See [Embedding Forms](./forms-embedding) for details.
:::

## Scheduling

Manage your booking pages and view all scheduled meetings. The scheduling section provides:
- **Booking Pages** — List of all meeting booking forms with status, duration, and booking counts
- **Bookings** — Table of all meetings with status filters (confirmed, cancelled, rescheduled, no-show)

Navigate to **Engagement → Scheduling** to manage your availability and bookings.

:::note
To create a new booking page, go to **Engagement → Forms** and click **New Booking Page**. Booking pages are a special type of form with calendar integration.
:::
