# Opportunity Detail Page

The opportunity detail page is a comprehensive hub for managing a deal. It brings together all the information, relationships, financials, and history you need to drive the deal to a successful close.

## Opportunity Detail Layout

The header section displays:

- **Opportunity Name** — displayed prominently
- **Amount** — the deal's monetary value
- **Stage Bar** — visual progress through pipeline stages (similar to leads)
- **Close Date** — expected closing date
- **Probability** — win likelihood percentage
- **Account** — linked organization
- **Owner** — assigned sales rep
- **Action buttons** — Edit, Close Won, Close Lost, Delete

![Screenshot: Opportunity detail page header showing name, amount, stage bar, and close date](../../static/img/screenshots/opportunities/opportunity-detail-header.png)

## Tabs

The opportunity detail page has **13 tabs** covering every aspect of deal management:

### Details
All standard and custom fields for the opportunity, organized into collapsible field groups. View and edit deal information, qualification criteria, and custom attributes.

### Contact Roles
Manage the stakeholders involved in this deal. Each contact is assigned a role (Decision Maker, Influencer, Champion, etc.).

To add a contact role:
1. Click **Add Contact Role**.
2. Search for a contact or create a new one.
3. Select the role from the dropdown.
4. Optionally mark as **primary contact**.
5. Click **Save**.

![Screenshot: Contact Roles tab showing stakeholders with their roles and a primary contact marked](../../static/img/screenshots/opportunities/contact-roles-tab.png)

:::tip
Ensure every opportunity has at least a Decision Maker and a Champion identified. Deals without clear stakeholder roles are statistically less likely to close.
:::

### Products
Line items for this opportunity — the products and services being sold.

To add a product:
1. Click **Add Product**.
2. Search for a product from your catalog.
3. Enter **quantity**, **unit price**, and any **discount**.
4. The line total calculates automatically.
5. Click **Save**.

The total of all line items can auto-update the opportunity amount.

### Proposals
Proposals generated for this opportunity. View, create, and track proposal documents sent to the prospect.

### Contracts
Contract records linked to this opportunity. Track contract status, terms, and signatures.

### Invoices
Invoices generated from this opportunity. Create invoices, track payment status, and view billing history. See [Invoices Overview](./invoices-overview.md).

### Activity
A timeline of all interactions related to this opportunity — calls, emails, meetings, stage changes, and system events. See [Activity Timeline](./activity-timeline.md).

### Emails
Email threads linked to this opportunity. Compose, reply, and forward emails in context.

### Notes
Free-text notes about the deal — meeting summaries, negotiation points, competitor intelligence, etc. See [Notes & Documents](./notes-documents.md).

### Files
Documents attached to this opportunity — proposals, contracts, presentations, RFPs, and supporting materials.

### Tasks
Tasks linked to this opportunity — follow-ups, demos, proposal reviews, contract preparation. Create tasks directly from this tab. See [Tasks Overview](./tasks-overview.md).

### Stage History
A chronological timeline of every stage change the opportunity has undergone:
- **Stage name** — which stage the opportunity moved to
- **Timestamp** — when the change occurred
- **Duration** — how long the opportunity spent in each stage
- **Changed by** — which user moved it

This provides visibility into deal velocity and identifies where deals get stuck.

![Screenshot: Stage History tab showing timeline of stage transitions with durations](../../static/img/screenshots/opportunities/stage-history.png)

### Forecast
Forecast-specific information for this opportunity:
- **Forecast Category** — how the deal is classified for forecasting
- **Weighted Amount** — amount multiplied by probability
- **Forecast History** — changes to forecast category over time

See [Forecasting](./opportunities-forecasting.md) for more details.

## Adding Contact Roles

Contact roles are critical for understanding who influences the deal. See the Contact Roles tab description above for step-by-step instructions.

## Managing Line Items

The Products tab provides full line-item management:
- **Add** products from the catalog
- **Edit** quantities, prices, and discounts
- **Remove** line items
- **Reorder** items by drag-and-drop
- View **subtotal, discount, tax, and grand total**

## Stage History Timeline

The Stage History tab provides accountability and insights:
- See which stages took the longest (potential bottlenecks)
- Identify who moved the deal at each step
- Understand deal velocity from creation to close

:::note
Stage history is automatically tracked by the system. Every stage change is recorded with no manual input required.
:::
